WorkProject

CAHELP Newsletters

I created and designed a newsletter for CAHELP, an organization within San Bernardino County Superintendent of Schools. These newsletters are distributed via email every month discussing what has been going on within the organization and highlights accessibility. I set up the emails to be distributed using mailchimp, a program that automates sending out the emails on a schedule. We have about 300 employees that receive Stay Connected newsletters and about 95% opens and clicks.
