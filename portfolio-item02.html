Personal Project

Akira Pill

I wanted to do something with the movie Akira. The movie is so iconic to this day in my opinion. The animation for its time was ground-breaking. The bike driven by Kaneda is unique in its design and makes its way into modern culture from time to time. For example, CD Projekt Red made the bike drivable in their game, Cyberpunk 2077.

For this project, I went in trying to figure out how noise texture can be utilized. That's what this project was for. I did my best to use noise texture and give the poster a unique type of look. I also threw in the phrase that can be seen on the back of Kaneda's jacket "Bad for Education, Good for Health" just to fit in with the theme a bit more.

Also, I was getting tired of making everything pass WCAG AA contrast issues at work so I made the title blend in a bit with the background and to make the pill stand out more.
