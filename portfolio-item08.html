WorkProject

Bingo Bash

For this project, the year was coming to an end and CAHELP holds an end of the year party for all staff to enjoy. With this, came the need for an admission ticket. The theme of the event was casinos and gambling.

With this information, I decided to go with a bingo style admission card. I wanted to make it pop with color so I made sure the colors stood out and that the center of the card caught your glance when you first look at it. With any project, certain information had to go on the card. The time, location and cost had to be on the card. This was one of those projects where it was a learning experience as I was still very new to Adobe Illustrator. However, in the end, I believe it came out well.
