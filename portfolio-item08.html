Court Interpreter - Certification Process


I was tasked with creating a video for future Court Interpreters. Since the process of attaining your certification requires many steps, I made a video showing the step by step process. I wanted to show it in a way that would want to make people stay and watch the whole video. Also, I really did not want to use any form of templates and animate everything myself to show that I am capable of creating animations and make it appealing to watch, which I succeeded in doing. I believe for this video we had an over 75% watch rate.
