Student Art Show

Recently, my school held an art show for students that were enrolled in art classes. The flyer they made contained square images of past student work, which was not very appealing to look at. I took it upon myself to redesign the flyer with relevant information. Now I need to mention that this flyer is not a real flyer used by the school. I only used my schools name and when the event was happening to fill out the flyer.

I have included a mockup flyer here.

This is an example of a Facebook post to go along with it that could be used to be posted on various social media sites.

Were it to be a special Student Art Show, I thought having admission tickets would be cool to have. I made them holographic to add to the novelty as well.
