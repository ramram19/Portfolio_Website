WorkProject

Superhero Flyer

I was tasked with creating a flyer that had to be superhero themed. My initial thoughts were to go into vector illustrations for the bulk of the design, but I knew this team would want to use pictures. With the superhero theme, I went into creating a moodboard to get some ideas down knowing where to go design-wise. Also, for the images, I had to be careful of my choices on what age range of children, ethnicities, and how cohesive the images had to be.
