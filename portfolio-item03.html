SchoolProject

Food Illustration

This was an assignment given to me that we had to select from a news article of each states most popular food dishes. A lot of people chose spaghetti, hotdogs, pasta, or hamburgers, but I wanted to do something a bit more challenging than those. I found this Slavic dish that serves pierogis with rooster shaped lollipops. Which, I thought was interesting and could be different enough to separate my design from everyone elses.

This second one took me a lot longer to finish because I had to individually select each shape to apply grain textures. Other than that I just based the pierogi off the one above and I used the same lollipop too.
