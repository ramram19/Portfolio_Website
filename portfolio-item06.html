SideProject

Zeta Tau Alpha

My friend is a part of the sorority, ZETA TAU ALPHA (ZTA), at CSUSB and asked if I could make some shirts for their sorority. No theme in mind for these, but she asked if they could include their colors as well as strawberries and white violets if it would be possible. I did come up with ten options and narrowed it down to the best five. Then from those five I asked them which would they like to see mocked up.

This one is more of a Y2k logo, which is what I was trying to go for for their sorority color, light blue, and also a white violet.

This shirt design was just a base shirt to see what they would like.
